Question

Can I use the CareLink Monitor (CLM) outside of the US (OUS)?

 

Response

Our US monitors should dial the US toll free number without any issues from Canada and Puerto Rico, but it will not work if the patient went to another country. However, some telecoms block toll free numbers. A patient who is traveling to Europe, or a country that uses the metric system, may need to know the baud rate of the CareLink Monitor (CLM). The baud rate of a data communications system is the number of symbols per second transferred. The CLM uses a V.34 modem which transmits at 3420 baud. Therefore, the Baud Rate of the CLM is 3420.
