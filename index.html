Question

How to set up CareAlert Notifications on CareLink?

 

Response

 

Details

You can customize how your clinic is notified when the Medtronic CareLink™ network receives a CareAlert™ transmission from a wireless device. You must have Administrative Privileges equal to Complete Privileges to configure CareAlert™ notifications for your clinic. DEFINE WIRELESS ALERT CONDITIONS The Alert Group tab is used to define wireless alert conditions for ICD, CRT, and Reveal LINQ™ devices. Any alert condition group setting may be overridden for an individual patient. You must have Administrative Privileges equal to Complete Privileges to configure CareAlert™ notifications for your clinic. Available alerts may vary by geography. CareAlert™ notifications require that the patient home monitor is programmed via the Medtronic programmer to “Yes.” Note: Alerts must be programmed in CRT/ICDs. Reveal LINQ™ devices do not require home monitoring to be programmed to “Yes”. Alerts for Reveal LINQ™ devices are controlled solely on the CareLink™ website. ICD Wireless Device Alert Conditions
