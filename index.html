Question

Where can I find the Setup Guide for the MyCarelink Smart?

 

Response

Use the attached Guide. 

 

Details

For further information, please see MyCareLink Smart Setup Guide.

 

Downloading the MCL Smart app:

  • Go to www.mycarelinksmart.com or open your app store and search mycarelink smart.
  • Select to download the app.
  • Turn your Bluetooth on.
  • Open the app and follow the instructions to pair your reader. The flashing light will go solid.
  • To transmit, press the green arrow.

Note: The MCL Smart app will update like any other app you put on your mobile device.

Once the app update is published in the app store:

  • If the user has the “automatically update apps” setting enabled on the mobile device then their app will update automatically.
  • If not, they will have to manually update from the app store as with any other app.
  • If the user hasn’t updated the app (and one is available) the app will pop-up a notification there is an app update available the next time they open it. There will be an option to navigate away or update the app in the pop-up message, similar to any other app.
  • The app can only be used with a MyCareLink Smart Reader. The reader is a portable electronic device that communicates with your implanted heart device.
