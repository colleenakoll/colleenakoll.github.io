Question

How to schedule transmissions on CareLink?

 

Response

See Details section for information on how to schedule transmissions on CareLink.

 

 

Details

You can view and schedule transmissions or delete scheduled transmissions of patient device data. For a new patient with a Conexus wireless telemetry-enabled device, an initial setup transmission is required to enable automatic monitoring. A minimum interval between scheduled transmissions is required for Conexus wireless telemetry-enabled devices. The Earliest Possible Transmission Date is the first date available for a patient’s next automatic transmission and is the date that the CareLink network expects a communication to occur. Existing transmission dates on the patient’s schedule may prevent the successful use of this date. If a transmission is required before this date, the patient can initiate a manual transmission. Note: Transmissions from Reveal LINQ devices are automatic as long as the patient monitor is plugged into an electrical outlet and there is adequate cellular signal. Scheduling a transmission for a Reveal LINQ patient does not result in a transmission. If the clinic wishes to have a Reveal LINQ patient send a transmission outside of their regular nightly transmission, the patient must be contacted and asked to send a manual transmission.

Step-by-step:

  • Open the Manage My Patients page.
  • Select a patient from the Patient List page.
  • Select the Schedule tab.
  • Enter a date for the scheduled transmission. Select the drop-down list in the Date column; then select a date from the pop-up calendar. Or, enter a date manually. Manually entered dates must be in the format DD-MMM-YYYY. The Day of Week and Interval columns will be populated for this appointment. The Save button is enabled.
  • Enter a Visit ID, if necessary. A Visit ID is an identifier used within the clinic to identify all interactions with a patient related to a single encounter. The Visit ID field is a 75-character text field and does  not have to be unique.
  • Enter additional appointments, if necessary. You can schedule up to six appointments at a time. When you enter an additional appointment, the Interval will be calculated from the previous scheduled appointment, even if that appointment has not been saved.
  • Click Save. A Confirmation message is displayed. 

ADVANCED SCHEDULING OPTIONS
You can schedule up to six weekly or monthly recurring transmissions for your patient.

Step-by-step:

  • Open the Manage My Patients page.
  • Select a patient from the Patient List.
  • Select the Schedule tab.
  • Click Advanced Scheduling Options.
  • Enter a Begin on date. The Begin on date defaults to the Earliest Possible Transmission Date available for a patient’s next automatic transmission and is the date that the CareLink™ network expects a communication to occur. Existing transmission dates on the patient’s schedule may prevent the successful use of this date. Select the drop-down list in the Date column; then select a date from the pop- up calendar. Or, enter a date manually. Manually entered dates must be in the format DD-MMM-YYYY.
  • Select a recurrence pattern in the Select pattern section. Select Weekly recurrence every: to schedule transmissions with a weekly recurrence pattern. Choose the number of weeks for the recurrence and select a day of the week for the transmission. Select Monthly recurrence every: to schedule transmissions with a monthly recurrence pattern. Choose the number of months and select a day of the week for the transmission.
  • Select the number of occurrences for the selected recurrence pattern from the occurrence(s) menu.
  • Click Save. The Advanced Scheduling Options window is closed.

SCHEDULING MULTIPLE PATIENTS
You can schedule multiple patients for transmissions on the same date.

Step-by-step:

  • Open the Manage My Patients page.
  • Click the check box next to the name of each patient you want to schedule.
  • Select Schedule.
  • If patients you selected can't be scheduled because they have a transmission scheduled that can't be deleted, an unsupported device, or don't have an active monitor prescription, they'll be removed from the list.
  • To schedule transmissions for these patients,listed above, use the Patient Detail page.
  • Enter a date for the scheduled transmission. Select the drop-down list in the Date column.
  • Select a date from the pop-up calendar or, enter a date manually. Manually entered dates must be in the format DD-MMM-YYYY. The Day of Week and Interval columns will be populated for this appointment. The Save button is enabled.
  • Click Save to schedule the appointment
