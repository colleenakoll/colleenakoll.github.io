Question

How often does the 2490C (Wireless) CareLink Monitor receive updates from the network?

Response

The FDN update only occurs during the maintenance session. On the date of the set-up transmission, the network will look for that monitor that night during the maintenance session.

Details

It will continue to look for it every 15 days during the routine maintenance sessions to update the monitor. The monitor will show successfully updated at the maintenance session following the successful session. (15 days later)

The wireless monitor will NEVER receive the firmware update with the manual transmission. The wireless monitors require an initial set-up transmission. Once this is completed the monitor should remain connected to AC power and connected to the telephone jack.
