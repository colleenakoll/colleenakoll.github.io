Question

What steps does a patient need to follow to set up and use their MyCareLink Smart Monitor (MCL-S)?

 

Response

Proper steps to setup and send a transmission with MCL-S:

  • Download the MCL Smart app
    • Go to www.mycarelinksmart.com or open your app store and search mycarelink smart.
    • Select to download the app.
  • Turn your Bluetooth on.
  • Open the app and follow the instructions to pair your reader. The flashing light will go solid.
  • To transmit, press the green arrow.

 

Notes:

  • The app can only be used with a MyCareLink Smart Reader. The reader is a portable electronic device that communicates with your implanted heart device.
  • The MCL Smart app will update like any other app you put on your mobile device. 

 

Details

For further information, please see Monitor Setup Guide for MyCareLink Smart.


Updating MCL-S app:

  • If “automatically update apps” setting is enabled then the app will update automatically.
  • If not, the app will pop-up a notification if an update is available next time the app is opened.
