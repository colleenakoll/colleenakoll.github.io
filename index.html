Question

How do I set up the MyCarelink Smart (MCL-S) reader and app?

 

Response

1. Place the batteries in the MCL-S reader.

2. Download the MCL-S app from mycarelinksmart.com.

3. Launch the MCL-S app on the smart device.

4. Press the green button on the screen to start the transmission.

5. Follow the prompts on the screen of the smart device.

 

Details

  • The screen will instruct the patient to press the gray power button on the top of the reader. (You may see a blue gear with arrows rotating around it.)
  • Enter the eight digit key code from the reader. (No spaces or dashes). Press the green button at the bottom of the screen to proceed.)
  • Patient should place the reader over their implant. A bar will show up across the screen and fill with green.
  • When finished, the smart device will beep and show a picture placing the reader down.
  • The next screen will be of the Medtronic app and a building with an H on it. The same bar will fill with green again. (This may not show up.)
  • The screen should say “Data Sent.”
