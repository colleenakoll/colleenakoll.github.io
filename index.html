Question

Why is there a busy signal when sending a transmission with my CareLink Monitor (CLM)?

 

Response

This may occur when the settings switches on the side of the monitor are not correct.

 

Details

Allow the CareLink Monitor (CLM) to turn itself off. Check switches and connection to the monitor, and disconnect the phone from the monitor’s extra jack. Reattempt transmission. The CLM will redial about four times normal sound dial tone and after the forth redial patient noticed the sending lights.
