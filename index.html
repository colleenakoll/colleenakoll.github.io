Question

How do I setup or change CareAlert Notifications for ICD, CRT, or Reveal LINQ patients on CareLink?

 

Response

You can customize how your clinic is notified when the Medtronic CareLink network receives a CareAlert transmission from a wireless device.  

The Alert Groups tab under CareAlert Notification Setup is used to define wireless alert conditions.  Available alerts may vary by geography. Alert conditions can be prioritized by configurable alert group settings: Red Alerts, Yellow Alerts, Website-Only Alerts, and No Alerts (LINQ only). For each alert condition, you can configure clinic alert groups that apply to all patients.

For individual patients, you can then override the clinic alert groups, as needed, with customized alerts for individual patients. Customized settings are assigned within the CareAlert Notification tab on the Patient Details page.

 

Notes:

  • User MUST HAVE Administrative Privileges equal to Complete Privileges to configure CareAlert Notifications for your clinic.
  • CareAlert notifications require that the patient home monitor is programmed via the Medtronic programmer to “Yes.” Alerts must be programmed in CRT/ICDs.
  • LINQ devices do not require home monitoring to be programmed to “Yes”. Alerts for LINQ devices are controlled solely on CareLink.

 

Details

To configure clinic-wide CareAlert condition groups on CareLink:

  • Open the Manage My Clinic page. If your clinic is in a network, select the appropriate clinic from the Clinic list.
  • Under CareAlert Notification Setup, select Alert Groups.
  • Select Display Alert Group Conditions to view and manage ICD Wireless Device Alert Conditions OR Reveal LINQ Device Alert Conditions.
  • For each Clinical Management Alert listed, select Red Alerts, Yellow Alerts, Website-only Alerts, or No Alerts (LINQ only).
  • For LINQ devices only
    • For AT/AF Daily Burden, enter the threshold for cumulative Time in AT/AF.
    • For Average Ventricular Rate during AT/AF, enter the threshold for cumulative Time in AT/AF above a specified average rate.
  • Click Save Changes.

 

 

To configure patient-specific CareAlert condition groups on CareLink:

  • Select the Manage My Patients page.
  • Search or select a patient from the Patient List.
  • Select the CareAlert Notification tab.
  • Select Override Clinic’s Alert Groups and customize for this patient.
  • For each Clinical Management Alert listed, select Red Alerts, Yellow Alerts, Website-only Alerts, or No Alerts (LINQ only).
  • For LINQ devices only
    • For AT/AF Daily Burden, enter the threshold for cumulative Time in AT/AF.
    • For Average Ventricular Rate during AT/AF, enter the threshold for cumulative Time in AT/AF above a specified average rate.
  • Click Save.

 

ICD Wireless Device Alert Conditions

 

SET UP RED OR YELLOW CAREALERT NOTIFICATION METHODS
You can specify daytime, after-hours and holiday notification methods for Red or Yellow CareAlerts for your clinic. Red or Yellow CareAlert notifications are used for Conexus wireless devices only. All Red alerts will trigger a color-coded website notification in the Alerts column on the transmission list and a description in the Event Summary column. Alerts are defined as red, yellow, or website only in the Alert Groups tab. You cannot set up customized notification methods for individual patients until your default notification methods have been set up on this screen. 

Step-by-step:

  • Open the Manage My Clinic page.
  • Under CareAlert notification setup, select Red or Yellow Alert Clinic Notification.
  • In the Daytime Hours Notification Methods section, choose how you would like to be notified during clinic business hours and complete all the related contact information boxes for each method.
  • In the After-Hours and Holiday Notification Methods section, choose how you would like to be notified after hours and on holidays.
  • Click Save.

SET UP NOTIFICATION HOURS
CareAlert notification hours can be set up for normal business hours, after-hours, and holidays. You must have Administrative Privileges equal to Complete Privileges to configure notification hours for your clinic. Holiday schedules need to be set up for the entire year and can be set up in advance for multiple years. The optional CareAlert Notification After-Hours and Holiday Notification Message Delay feature delays notification of Red and Yellow alerts from Conexus wireless devices to your clinic that occur after-hours or on holidays. Transmissions that meet the established alert conditions display on the CareLink website, but your clinic will not receive the advanced alert notification until the delay period passes.

Step-by-step:

  • Open the Manage My Clinic page.
  • Under CareAlert notification setup, select Notification Hours.
  • Select each normal business day for your clinic from the Daytime Hours section. Any time set outside the hours listed in the Daytime Hours section is considered After-Hours. For each business day, use the From box to enter the time that your clinic opens and the To box to enter the time that your clinic closes.
  • To delay Red or Yellow Advanced CareAlert notifications for events that occur after-hours or on holidays, select an option from the After-Hours and Holiday Notification Message Delay section.
    • To delay notification of Red Alerts, select Delay sending my clinic notification methods for Red Alert transmissions from the Red Alerts section. Select the start time to delay notification transmissions from the Delay notification messages received after menu. Select the end time from the Until the next day at menu.
    • To delay notification of Yellow Alerts, select Delay sending my clinic notification methods for Yellow Alert transmissions from the Yellow Alerts section. Select the start time to delay notification transmissions from the Delay notification messages received after menu. Select the end time from the Until the next day at menu.
  • To specify clinic holidays, enter a Holiday Name in the Holidays section. Click Add another Holiday to add additional holidays when your clinic is not open. Select a date for each holiday from the Date menu.
  • Click Save.

 

TEST NOTIFICATION METHODS
You can test the optional notification methods set up for Red and Yellow CareAlert notifications. This test verifies that you have entered the information correctly and that the notifications transmit properly. You must have Administrative Privileges equal to Complete Privileges to test notification methods.

 Step-by-step:

  • Open the Manage My Clinic page.
  • Under CareAlert notification setup, select either the Red Alert Clinic Notification or Yellow Alert Clinic Notification link.
  • Click the Test Notification Methods link for either the Daytime Hours Notification Methods or After-Hours and Holiday Notification Methods.
  • The system sends a test CareAlert notification message to the destination specified.

 

CAREALERT NOTIFICATIONS FOR REVEAL LINQ ICM
Consider enabling the following CareAlert notifications to receive information that may be near term or immediately actionable in your care plan for each patient. The decision to enable CareAlert notifications should be weighed against the work flow of your clinic to receive and analyze reports. Data collected wirelessly during the month is available on each patient’s automatically generated Summary Report.

REVEAL LINQ WIRELESS DEVICE ALERT CONDITIONS
Any alert condition group setting may be overridden for an individual patient. Customized settings are assigned within the Patient Profile.
