Question

How to change the Clinic Group Administrator (CGA) on CareLink?

 

Response

See Details section for more information on how to change the CGA on CareLink.

 

Details

 

To change CGA or primary contact on CareLink:

  • Open the Manage My Clinic page.
  • Click Clinic Profile.
  • Select the administrator for your clinic from the CareLink™ administrator list.
  • Use the Primary Contact section to enter appropriate information for your primary contact. This name will be displayed as the contact for your clinic when using the transfer patient function.
  • Click Save.

 

Note: 

  • You MUST HAVE Complete Privileges to change CGA on CareLink. Only users with Complete Priviledges will appear on CareLink Administrator list.
