Question

What are the monitor specifications and electrical costs for the CareLink Monitor (CLM)?

 

Response

The approximate (not exact) monitor dimensions of the CareLink Monitor (CLM):

2490G/H/J - 10" x 8.5" x 3"

2490C - 10" x 8.5" x 3.25"

 

Temperature limits of the CareLink Monitor (CLM) for 2490 G/H/J/ and C's:

Storage -40 to 150 degrees Fahrenheit (-40 to 65 degrees Celsius)

Operating 49F to 110 degrees Fahrenheit (9 to 43 degrees Celsius)

 

The approximate electrical cost of keeping the CLM plugged in all the time is about 1/3 of a cent per day, or $0.88 annually.
